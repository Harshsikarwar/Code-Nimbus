Projects

MediLink – Healthcare Appointment & Records Platform

Client: A mid-sized healthcare chain We developed a secure, cloud-based platform that allows patients to book appointments, access medical history, and consult doctors online. Integrated with EHR systems and HIPAA-compliant, MediLink modernized the client’s patient experience and reduced admin workload by 40%.

ShopVerse – Custom E-Commerce Ecosystem

Client: A boutique retail brand expanding online We built a fully customized e-commerce platform with multi-vendor support, real-time inventory, and a personalized recommendation engine. From seamless checkout to customer analytics, ShopVerse helped increase online sales by 75% in just 6 months.

Learnova – E-Learning Management System (LMS)

Client: An online education startup Learnova is a modern LMS with interactive video modules, gamification, progress tracking, and virtual classrooms. Built for scalability and mobile-first access, it now serves over 10,000 learners across 3 countries.
